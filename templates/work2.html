Breast Cancer Diagnostic System

Breast cancer is the second most common cancer with a recorded 2.09 million cases and 627 000 deaths in 2018 according to the World Health Organization (WHO). Early diagnosis significantly increases chances of survival. The key challenge in cancer detection is how to classify tumors into malignant or benign. Use of Artificial Intelligence and Machine learning techniques can dramatically improve the accuracy of diagnosis by upto 98%.
