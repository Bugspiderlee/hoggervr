What is blockchaining?


A blockchain is a decentralized, distributed and oftentimes a public digital ledger consisting of records called blocks that is used to record transactions across many computers so that any involved block cannot be altered retroactively, without the alteration of all subsequent blocks. The same technology can be used to protect almost any form of data. At Hoggervr we us it for hospital patient data protection.
