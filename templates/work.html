AI Based COVID-19 Induced

Pneumonia Diagnostic System


Our aim is to help radiologists with tools in this COVID-19 pandemic as more pneumonia cases are present which require rapid and accurate diagnosis. This will help as a severity score analytic tool to risk stratify patients with faster detection and isolation of patients without compromising the quality of care. We developed an artificial intelligence algorithm to differentiate between COVID-19 induced pneumonia and non-COVID-19 pneumonia by feature extraction on chest x-rays using deep neural network techniques with an accuracy which can reach up to 98.88%.
